Want to know more about us?





We are the official student automotive team of SRM Institute of Science and Technology. Scholars from different branches of engineering work together to fabricate a fully functioning solar car.

Over the past few years we have participated in national level solar championships like ESVC, NSVC, IASC and SUVC. As a team, we have worked hard in taking SRM's name to new grounds and have managed to build a great reputation in the competitions participated in and also around the campus.
